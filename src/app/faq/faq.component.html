Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Completely up to you, but please remember not to wear white like the bride :)

Are children invited?

Of course! For us, all our guests are like family.

Is there parking nearby?

Both the ceremony and the reception are held in the center of Granada, so parking will be difficult or in restricted areas. We recommend checking the parking section on this website to see which ones we suggest.

Will there be transportation to the reception?

The church is about a 10-minute walk from the reception venue, so there will be no transportation. If anyone has mobility issues, please let us know in the attendance form.

Is the reception venue accessible for people with reduced mobility?

The hotel is fully accessible for people with reduced mobility. However, if you need additional assistance, please let us know so we can help you.
